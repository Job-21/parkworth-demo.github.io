Our Vision

About us

To be a key player in Civil Construction specializing in Civil Engineering Environment by giving quality service to our clients.


This vision will be achieved by:

  • Creating an environment that will attract, retain and develop the best employees.
  • Delivery of quality products and services timeously to clients.
  • Flexibility and innovation to meet varying client needs.
  • Managing relationships with clients and other stakeholders with professionalism and integrity.
  • Meeting social, health, safety and environmental responsibilities.

Mission Statement

MISSION

PARK WORTH INFRASTRUCTURE ENGINEERING LTD Company is a leader in providing value added construction services to our clients by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our clients by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of our construction team.

CORE VALUES OF THE COMPANY

CORE VALUES

PWIEC has the following core values:

  1. Quality
    Quality is key for PWIEC. We aim to make sure that the expectations of our clients and all stakeholders are fully complied with and that projects are delivered with a high standard of workmanship in a timely manner. We always use products and materials that meet or exceed the requirements of the contract documents and ensure our staff are qualiﬁed and well trained.
    14
  2. Integrity
    PWIEC ensures honesty, strong moral principles, and moral uprightness in conduct of business. It has an ethical code of conduct that we use in the selection and training of our staff and stakeholders.
    14
  3. Health and Safety
    The health, safety and welfare of all staff, sub-contractors, clients and visitors is signiﬁcantly important to PWIEC and it is our intention to strive for excellence in the effective health and safety management of all construction sites under our control. We also aim to effectively mitigate all hazards arising from our facilities, activities and site-speciﬁc environments. We also ensure we comply with all legal obligations, relevant standards and codes of practice with a commitment to developing a culture of safety with in the civil construction industry.
    14
  4. Professionalism
    We demonstrate commitment to our contracts, stick to deadlines and standards. We also treat our clients and stakeholders with respect, keep our word and exceed expectations.
    14
  5. Environmental Sustainability
    At PWIEC we understand that environmental sustainability is everyone’s responsibility and we are prepared to lead the way in this regard. We also realize that a commitment to the environment is not just social responsibility, but also good business practice as we seek to increase our constantly growing reputation. For this reason, we ensure that our environment policy is implemented throughout every facet of our business. Our overall objective is to carry out our operations in a way which manages and minimizes any adverse effects on the environment.
