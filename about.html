Who we are

About us

PWIEC was incorporated in 12th August, 2015 by a group of young and passionate Ugandans. Following their successful participation in a number of projects, they started the company to also make a contribution to the infrastructure development in Uganda. The founders are aware that infrastructure is the next big driver for growth across Africa and are sure that Uganda will not be left behind. We are yearning to be part of this growth revolution and ready to make a contribution to the development of infrastructure that will transform Uganda and Africa at large. We are targeting to work for Central Government Agencies, Local Governments, NGOs, International Organizations and private sector companies. We would like to be an infrastructure development partner, that will deliver projects with the expected quality and in time. We appreciate the importance of being a reliable company that will connect Uganda and trigger the economy to beneﬁt from delivery of good infrastructure projects. It has been said for long that Uganda lacks local capacity to deliver on infrastructure projects and PWIEC started with that in mind, it has also been said that local infrastructure ﬁrm owners lack the discipline to use the resources given to them on projects to deliver good quality. The founders also appreciate that and make a historical commitment to use the resources got from clients to deliver exactly what is speciﬁed.

We intend to seek local and international players to partner with in order to achieve our goal of contributing to the development of our country, we will seek to use modern technology that will enable us blend modern infrastructure designs and environmental sustainability. We are also sensitive to human rights issues while handling projects. These include; equal employment opportunities such as giving women to take a chance in projects we work on. We also strongly desist from employing children on project sites because we know that children should be in school.

Being a responsible local content player, we strongly believe and support the call to increase participation of local ﬁrms and individuals in projects and we will be responsible players that fulﬁll the expectation of the government and other local clients.

PARK WORTH INFRASTRUCTURE ENGINEERING LTD Company is a leader in providing value added construction services to our clients by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our clients by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of our construction team.
