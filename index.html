Our Overview

Overview

Working from offices strategically located in major metropolitan areas in Africa and abroad, Parkworth Infrastructure Engineering Co. Ltd (PWIEC) has in its several years of practice, developed a diverse portfolio of construction to serve clients in any array of business markets.

PWIEC has secured partnerships with Pristine Technology Resources in Malaysia and Geosign signage Solutions in Italy with endorsement for its proﬁciency, consistency and ﬁner quality of work. The company has today handled large construction projects mainly in Road sector and Buildings sector. The company expects to acquire opportunities offered in global market also.

PWIEC professionals know that every planning, architecture, engineering, infrastructure or construction project requires a different knowledge set to successfully address essential issues and challenges. Our teams of specialist’s aid clients in discovering important factors that could potentially provide project obstacles such as geographic challenges, aesthetics and required work place efficiencies.
